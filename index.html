About Iveno Carolus

Iveno Carolus is an accomplished Data Scientist with a rich background in data analysis, programming, machine learning, and data engineering. His fascination with coding began at the age of 12, driven by a passion for creating software that learns, which laid the foundation for his career in Data Science. With over a decade of experience in Python, including four years of professional expertise, and a strong command of cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, and GCP, Iveno excels in building and managing data pipelines and systems.

His professional journey began with a Data Science internship at Ignition Group, where he gained critical skills in data analytics and machine learning. As a Junior Data Scientist at the same company, he expanded his expertise in big data visualization and cloud-based data solutions. At Sprout Performance Partners, he honed his skills in automating data processes and analyzing digital marketing data using GCP and GMP, significantly improving campaign strategies and operational efficiency. His subsequent role as an independent contractor at Cavell Group involved developing skills for highly complex scraping pipelines with Azure and Python, as well as delving into the use of large language models (LLMs) for data summarization and extraction of key insights. Additionally, he conducted research in natural language processing (NLP), further refining his expertise in these advanced areas.

Currently, as a freelance Data Scientist and Software Developer, Iveno has developed a Micro Loan Web App for Maroon Capital, showcasing his proficiency in Django and ReactJS. His previous roles also include automating data processes and optimizing digital marketing strategies, demonstrating his adaptability and problem-solving skills in the dynamic field of Data Science.
